HC Euro 6 in 1 laundry detergent is a new product on a modern technological line and innovative formula to bring maximum convenience to users.

HC Euro laundry detergent is increasingly chosen by many families because it has outstanding features that are difficult to find in a regular laundry detergent.

Product uses:

Penetrates deep into fabric fibers to clean clothes, remove stubborn stains, leaving clothes clean and fragrant.
Breakthrough formula with active ingredients to whiten clothes, without affecting fabric quality, does not wear out fabric, very safe
Fabric softener essence, helps clothes become soft, smooth, gentle on the skin, and helps increase the life of clothes, keeping clothes always new, without yellowing or losing color.
High antibacterial properties help prevent mold and unpleasant odors during the rainy season.
Leaves an extremely pleasant, fresh scent all day long.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place

Out of reach of children

3 years from the date printed on the product packaging.

Instructions for use:
Hand wash

Mix (20 - 30ml) of detergent into 5 liters of water for about 5 sets of clothes, soak for 10 minutes before washing, wring gently and rinse with water, then dry.

Machine wash

Mix (20 - 30ml) of detergent with 8 liters of water in the washing machine, set the washing mode corresponding to the amount of clothes to be washed.
